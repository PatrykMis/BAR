BAR is a simple, local-first audio recorder for Android, built for reliable background recording.

Start recording from a Quick Settings tile, keep using your phone normally, and pause or stop from the persistent notification. BAR saves standard audio files in a folder you choose.

No account. No ads. No tracking. No cloud upload. No network permission.

Features:
- Quick Settings tile for starting and stopping recordings
- Reliable background recording while using other apps
- Local file storage with Android's folder picker
- OGG/Opus, M4A/AAC, FLAC, and WAV output
- Mono/stereo, sample rate, source, and bit depth options where supported
- File retention option
- Advanced diagnostics for sample rate testing
- Designed to work well with screen readers

Limitations:
- BAR records microphone audio, not phone calls.
- During phone calls, Android may mute microphone recording because of platform privacy restrictions.
